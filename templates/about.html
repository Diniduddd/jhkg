JHKG is a Canadian competition in computer programming. It is the spiritual successor to DWITE, and a great way to prepare for the CCC.

Format

We'll have five contests. September, October, November, January, and February. Then we rest.

Each contest will have four problems in roughly increasing difficulty: two easy ones, two hard ones, and an impossible one that must be approximated.

This may change at any time.
