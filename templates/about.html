Welcome!

JHKG is a Canadian competition in computer programming. It is the spiritual successor to DWITE, and a great way to practice for the CCC.

Format

(This may change.)

We'll have five contests. October, November, December, January, and February. Then we do the CCC, then we rest.

Each contest will have four problems in roughly increasing difficulty.

Problems

When you are ready to submit a solution, press the button and you will be given some input. You will have four minutes to feed it to your program and paste the output in the other box.

Scoring

Solving a problem is worth 100 points. There will also be a time bonus of up to 100 points.

History

We're just two guys who met at CCC Stage 2. That's all~!
